About the Video

The main line of the video is about the background music 宇宙に行ったライオン - The Lion Who Went into Space. The song talks about a loin who escaped from the circuit and embraced the world. He was lonely and arragont at the beginning until he met his companion, a boy. They travelled around the world and then have higher desitinations.

It's one of my favourite band songs from kanjani8. I was couraged by the wide world view involved in the song. Kanjani has played the song at multiple concerts. I think it's lovely to collect and edit the videos to remake the mv based on all those music clips for the song from my point of view. Also, I'd like to introduce how cool Kanjani8 is when playing band! I wish people who viewed the video can enjoy the song and the band's performance.
