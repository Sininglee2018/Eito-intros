Height: 176cm

Weight: 62.3kg

Blood A

Talent agency: Johnny's Entertainment



personal information

Ryuhei Maruyama took the audition of Johnny & Associates on September 1996 due to his parents recommended to take it. However, he didn’t hear anything after audition so he thought he was failed. On September 1997, he took another audition of the company again, but at the time, the director Johnny Kitazawa told him “Why are you here though you are already passed this audition last time”. He joined Kanjani8 in 2002 and debuted in 2004 with a song of “Naniwa Iroha Bushi”.
